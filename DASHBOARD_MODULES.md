# Legal Compliance Operations Dashboard Modules

The combined dashboard includes:

- Matter intake and deadline risk
- Contract review and clause risk
- Legal holds and eDiscovery batches
- Filing packets and deadlines
- Compliance controls and policy evidence
- Audit findings and remediation
- Legal reports and AI assistant review

Dashboard rule:
- no source-project dashboards are exposed directly
- donor dashboards are represented as dashboard modules inside this unified suite
